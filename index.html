Castora Foam Tech · Bhavnagar, Gujarat
01 · Castings

Lost Foam Casting, Engineered for Scale

Castora Foam Tech is a precision Lost Foam Casting (LFC) manufacturer producing near-net-shape steel castings for container, automotive, railway, and industrial applications. A 40,000 sq ft facility in Bhavnagar runs the full process in-house — from EPS pattern moulding through induction melting to inspection and dispatch — with full batch traceability throughout.

Core Capabilities
  • Seven-stage Lost Foam Casting process line
  • 500–550 kW induction melting, 1,500°C+
  • In-house spectro, hardness & NDT testing lab
  • 98% sand reclamation efficiency
  • Near-net-shape, complex geometry castings
  • ISO-compliant castings across multiple product lines
Industries Served
Container Manufacturing Shipping & Port Operations Automotive & Tractor Pump & Valve Manufacturing Railway
300 MT monthly casting capacity across a fully equipped production line
Full batch traceability with in-house metallurgical testing
Strategic Bhavnagar location for fast dispatch to Indian ports

Heavy Engineering & Structural Fabrication

Param Engineers is a heavy engineering fabricator near Vadodara, building industrial structures, storage tanks, silos, furnaces, and plant equipment across MS, SS and specialised alloys — with fabrication, machining, testing and finishing carried out under one roof.

Core Capabilities
  • Structural steel & industrial plant fabrication
  • Storage silos, vessels, tanks & hoppers
  • Industrial furnaces, chimneys & transformer tanks
  • CNC plasma cutting (up to 14,000×3,200mm) & plate rolling
  • Fabrication across MS, SS, brass, copper, bronze & alloys
  • Hydraulic & pneumatic pressure testing in-house
Industries Served

APPL Containers · Bhavnagar, Gujarat
04 · Shipping Containers

ISO Shipping Container Manufacturing

APPL Containers is among India's first ISO shipping container manufacturers, producing over 15,000 containers annually from a modern facility in Bhavnagar — inaugurated in 2022 in support of the "Make in India" initiative.

Core Capabilities

  • Standard, High Cube & Open Top containers
  • Specialised BESS, cement-tank & coil containers
  • In-house testing rig & dedicated R&D department
  • ISO 1496-compliant, TUV NORD verified
  • Custom sizing, coatings & door configurations
